What does a ticket get you?

A single admission ticket is a weekend pass to all of the events we run at the Maelstrom Mixer, as well as a swag bag.

Friday 5:00pm-9:30PMA low-stakes, casual Kill Team Event. In addition, access to open gaming on FL 40K’s tables. Friday’s events are kept loose so people can jump in and participate (or not) as close to their arrival time as possible after fighting with Bay Area traffic!

Saturday 10:00AM-7:00PMParticipation in one of two simultaneously running narrative events. Each event has a morning round and an afternoon round with time for lunch in between. Open Kill Team Tables and some 40K Board Games floating around too for those who finish a round early!

Sunday 10:00AM-7:00PMParticipation in one of two simultaneously running narrative events. Each event has a morning round and an afternoon round with time for lunch in between. Open Kill Team Tables and some 40K Board Games floating around too for those who finish a round early!

Saturday, Sunday, 7:00PM+After hours hang/dinner/drinks organized by Faultline at neighboring bars and restaurants. Food and drink not included in ticket cost.
